Welcome

Cultural-awareness learning through immersive simulation and reflection

Step into realistic workplace moments, explore different perspectives, and reflect on how your choices can shape culture, inclusion, and empathy.

  • Switch roles in scenario “rooms” that feel like VR spaces.
  • Practice handling bias, power, and sensitive situations safely.
  • Learn at your own pace – no real HR decisions are made here.
How it works
1) Choose how you’re using Trading Places.
2) Pick a scenario space from your dashboard.
3) Move through scenes, make choices, and reflect.

Accessibility & how you’re using Trading Places

Choose any options that support a more comfortable and inclusive experience. Settings are stored locally on this device and can be changed later.
